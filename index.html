Key Factors Affecting Crime with Accessible Data

Analyzing various socio-economic factors to understand their impact on crime rates.

1. Crime Rates per State

Description: Measures the number of reported crimes in each state.

Data Sources:

  • India: National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB)
  • USA: FBI’s Uniform Crime Reporting (UCR) Program
  • Other Countries: Respective national or regional law enforcement agencies.

2. Number of Open Areas per State

Description: Counts of parks, recreational spaces, and public gardens.

Data Sources:

  • OpenStreetMap (OSM): Use Overpass Turbo to query and extract data on public parks and open spaces.
  • Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change (India): MoEFCC
  • State Urban Planning Departments: Check individual state websites for specific data on public open areas.

3. Unemployment Rate per State

Description: Percentage of the labor force that is unemployed in each state.

Data Sources:

  • India: Ministry of Labour & Employment
  • USA: Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS)
  • Other Countries: National statistics offices (e.g., ONS for the UK).
